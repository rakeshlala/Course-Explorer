B.Sc. in Nutrition & Dietetics

1) What is B.Sc. in Nutrition & Dietetics?

B.Sc. in Nutrition & Dietetics is an undergraduate program that focuses on human nutrition, diet planning, and food science. It trains students to help individuals and communities maintain healthy lifestyles through proper diet and nutrition advice.

2) Key Features of B.Sc. in Nutrition & Dietetics

Full Form

B.Sc. in Nutrition & Dietetics.

Duration

3 years.

Eligibility

12th Science stream (preferably with Biology), usually with at least 50% aggregate marks.

Top Colleges in India

  • Lady Irwin College, Delhi
  • Mount Carmel College, Bangalore
  • SNDT Women's University, Mumbai
  • University of Delhi
  • Amity University, Noida

Admission

Based on 12th marks (merit-based) or entrance exams conducted by some universities.

Fees

₹30,000 – ₹1,50,000 per year (varies by college).

Career Opportunities

  • Dietitian
  • Nutritionist
  • Clinical Dietitian
  • Food Quality Manager
  • Public Health Nutritionist
  • Further studies (M.Sc., PhD, etc.)

Salary in India

₹3 lakh – ₹6 lakh per year (starting); increases with experience and specialization.
