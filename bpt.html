BPT (Bachelor of Physiotherapy)

1) What is BPT?

BPT (Bachelor of Physiotherapy) is an undergraduate program focused on physical therapy and rehabilitation techniques. It trains students to help patients restore mobility, reduce pain, and improve overall physical function after injuries or medical conditions.

2) Key Features of BPT

Full Form

Bachelor of Physiotherapy.

Duration

4.5 years (including 6 months of mandatory internship).

Eligibility

12th Science stream (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), usually with at least 50% aggregate marks.

Top Colleges in India

  • Manipal College of Allied Health Sciences, Manipal
  • Christian Medical College (CMC), Vellore
  • Amity University, Noida
  • SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Chennai
  • Jamia Hamdard, New Delhi

Admission

Based on entrance exams or merit (12th marks), depending on the college.

Fees

₹50,000 – ₹2,50,000 per year (varies by college).

Career Opportunities

  • Physiotherapist in hospitals and clinics
  • Rehabilitation Specialist
  • Sports Physiotherapist
  • Orthopedic Physiotherapist
  • Academician or Lecturer
  • Further studies (MPT, specialization, etc.)

Salary in India

₹3 lakh – ₹7 lakh per year (starting); increases with experience and specialization.

3) BPT Admission Process (Steps)

  • Step 1: Check eligibility (12th with Biology).
  • Step 2: Apply to colleges/universities.
  • Step 3: Appear for entrance exam (if required).
  • Step 4: Check merit list or exam result.
  • Step 5: Attend counseling and document verification.
  • Step 6: Pay fees and confirm admission.

Summary

BPT is a 4.5-year undergraduate program that prepares students to diagnose and treat physical disabilities and injuries through physiotherapy techniques. It offers rewarding career opportunities in hospitals, sports centers, rehabilitation facilities, and private practice.
