B.Stat (Bachelor of Statistics)

1) What is B.Stat?

B.Stat (Bachelor of Statistics) is an undergraduate program that focuses on statistical methods, data analysis, probability, and mathematics. It trains students to analyze complex data and solve real-world problems using statistical tools.

2) Key Features of B.Stat

Full Form

Bachelor of Statistics.

Duration

3 years.

Eligibility

12th with Mathematics and a strong background in quantitative subjects; usually with at least 60% aggregate marks.

Top Colleges in India

  • Indian Statistical Institute (ISI), Kolkata (most prestigious)
  • Delhi University
  • St. Xavier’s College, Mumbai
  • Loyola College, Chennai
  • Madras Christian College, Chennai

Admission

Primarily through entrance exams (e.g., ISI Admission Test) and interviews in some colleges.

Fees

₹20,000 – ₹1,00,000 per year (varies by college).

Career Opportunities

  • Statistician
  • Data Analyst
  • Risk Analyst
  • Market Researcher
  • Actuary
  • Further studies (M.Stat, M.Sc. Statistics, Data Science, etc.)

Salary in India

₹4 lakh – ₹8 lakh per year (starting); increases significantly with experience and specialization.

3) B.Stat Admission Process (Steps)

  • Step 1: Check eligibility (12th with Mathematics).
  • Step 2: Apply for entrance exams (e.g., ISI Admission Test).
  • Step 3: Appear for written test and interview (if required).
  • Step 4: Check merit list or selection result.
  • Step 5: Attend counseling and document verification.
  • Step 6: Pay fees and confirm admission.
