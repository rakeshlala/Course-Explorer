BMLT (Bachelor in Medical Laboratory Technology)

1) What is BMLT?

BMLT (Bachelor in Medical Laboratory Technology) is an undergraduate program focused on diagnostic laboratory technologies, clinical testing, and medical laboratory management. It trains students to work as skilled lab technologists in hospitals and diagnostic labs.

2) Key Features of BMLT

Full Form

Bachelor in Medical Laboratory Technology.

Duration

3 years.

Eligibility

12th Science stream (with Biology, Chemistry, and Physics), usually with at least 50% aggregate marks.

Top Colleges in India

  • All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi
  • Christian Medical College (CMC), Vellore
  • Manipal Academy of Higher Education, Manipal
  • Amity University, Noida
  • Jamia Hamdard, New Delhi

Admission

Based on merit (12th marks) or entrance exams conducted by universities/colleges.

Fees

₹50,000 – ₹2,00,000 per year (varies by college).

Career Opportunities

  • Medical Laboratory Technologist
  • Pathology Lab Technician
  • Clinical Laboratory Manager
  • Blood Bank Technologist
  • Research Lab Technician
  • Further studies (MMLT, M.Sc., etc.)

Salary in India

₹2.5 lakh – ₹5 lakh per year (starting); increases with experience and specialization.

3) BMLT Admission Process (Steps)

  • Step 1: Check eligibility (12th Science with Biology).
  • Step 2: Apply to colleges/universities.
  • Step 3: Appear for entrance exam (if required).
  • Step 4: Check merit list or exam result.
  • Step 5: Attend counseling and document verification.
  • Step 6: Pay fees and confirm admission.

Summary

BMLT is a 3-year undergraduate program designed to prepare students for roles in medical diagnostic labs, hospitals, and research labs. It offers solid career opportunities and options for further specialization in medical and clinical laboratory sciences.
