B.Com (Hons.)

1) What is B.Com (Hons.)?

B.Com (Hons.) or Bachelor of Commerce (Honours) is a 3-year undergraduate program that offers an in-depth and specialized study of commerce, finance, accounting, and economics. It is more focused and advanced compared to the regular B.Com program.

2) Key Features of B.Com (Hons.)

Full Form

Bachelor of Commerce (Honours).

Duration

3 years.

Eligibility

12th in any stream (preferably Commerce), usually with at least 55% aggregate marks. Some universities may require Mathematics as a subject.

Top Colleges in India

  • Shri Ram College of Commerce (SRCC), Delhi University
  • Lady Shri Ram College, Delhi University
  • Hindu College, Delhi University
  • St. Xavier’s College, Kolkata
  • Christ University, Bangalore

Admission

Based on merit (12th marks) or entrance exams (like DUET for Delhi University and others).

Fees

₹25,000 – ₹2,00,000 per year (varies by college).

Career Opportunities

  • Chartered Accountant (CA)
  • Financial Analyst
  • Investment Banker
  • Tax Consultant
  • Corporate Analyst
  • Further studies (MBA, M.Com, CFA, etc.)

Salary in India

₹4 lakh – ₹8 lakh per year (starting); increases significantly with professional certifications and experience.

3) B.Com (Hons.) Admission Process (Steps)

  • Step 1: Check eligibility (12th passed with required percentage).
  • Step 2: Apply to universities/colleges offering B.Com (Hons.).
  • Step 3: Appear for entrance exams (if required).
  • Step 4: Check merit list or exam result.
  • Step 5: Attend counseling and document verification.
  • Step 6: Pay fees and confirm admission.

Summary

B.Com (Hons.) is a specialized 3-year undergraduate program focusing on advanced studies in commerce and finance. It is ideal for students who aim to pursue high-level careers in finance, accounting, business analysis, and related fields, or who plan to pursue professional courses like CA or MBA.
