BBS (Bachelor of Business Studies)

1) What is BBS?

BBS (Bachelor of Business Studies) is a 3-year undergraduate program focused on business management, entrepreneurship, marketing, finance, and organizational skills. It aims to develop versatile managers and business leaders.

2) Key Features of BBS

Full Form

Bachelor of Business Studies.

Duration

3 years.

Eligibility

12th in any stream (preferably Commerce), usually with at least 50% aggregate marks. Some universities may require Mathematics or Economics as preferred subjects.

Top Colleges in India

  • Shaheed Sukhdev College of Business Studies (SSCBS), Delhi University
  • Deen Dayal Upadhyaya College, Delhi University
  • Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi
  • Other Delhi University affiliated colleges

Admission

Based on entrance exams (like DU JAT for Delhi University) and/or 12th merit, depending on the college.

Fees

₹30,000 – ₹1,50,000 per year (varies by college).

Career Opportunities

  • Business Analyst
  • Marketing Executive
  • Sales Manager
  • Operations Manager
  • Human Resource Executive
  • Further studies (MBA, PGDM, etc.)

Salary in India

₹3 lakh – ₹6 lakh per year (starting); higher packages possible with experience and further studies.

3) BBS Admission Process (Steps)

  • Step 1: Check eligibility (12th passed with required percentage).
  • Step 2: Apply to universities/colleges offering BBS.
  • Step 3: Appear for entrance exam (if required).
  • Step 4: Check merit list or exam result.
  • Step 5: Attend counseling and document verification.
  • Step 6: Pay fees and confirm admission.

Summary

BBS is a 3-year undergraduate management program designed to prepare students for managerial and entrepreneurial roles. It builds a strong foundation in business studies and opens doors for further studies like MBA or specialized management courses.
