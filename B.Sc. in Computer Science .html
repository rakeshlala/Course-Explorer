B.Sc. in Computer Science

1) What is B.Sc. in Computer Science?

B.Sc. in Computer Science is an undergraduate program focused on the study of computer systems, programming, software development, and data analysis. It prepares students for careers in IT and software industries.

2) Key Features of B.Sc. in Computer Science

Full Form

B.Sc. in Computer Science — Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.

Duration

3 years.

Eligibility

12th Science stream (with Mathematics), usually with at least 50% aggregate marks.

Top Colleges in India

  • St. Stephen’s College, Delhi
  • Christ University, Bangalore
  • Loyola College, Chennai
  • St. Xavier’s College, Mumbai
  • Fergusson College, Pune

Admission

Based on 12th marks (merit-based) or entrance exams conducted by certain universities.

Fees

₹30,000 – ₹2,00,000 per year (varies by college).

Career Opportunities

  • Software Developer
  • Web Developer
  • Data Analyst
  • Systems Analyst
  • IT Support Specialist
  • Further studies (M.Sc., MCA, etc.)

Salary in India

₹3 lakh – ₹7 lakh per year (starting); increases significantly with experience and specialization.

3) B.Sc. in Computer Science Admission Process (Steps)

  • Step 1: Check eligibility (12th with Mathematics).
  • Step 2: Apply to colleges/universities.
  • Step 3: Appear for entrance exam (if required).
  • Step 4: Check merit list or exam result.
  • Step 5: Attend counseling and document verification.
  • Step 6: Pay fees and confirm admission.

Summary
