B.Sc. in Agriculture

1) What is B.Sc. in Agriculture?

B.Sc. in Agriculture is an undergraduate degree focusing on agriculture and related sciences. It covers topics such as farming techniques, soil science, plant breeding, animal husbandry, agricultural economics, and modern agribusiness methods.

2) Key Features of B.Sc. in Agriculture

Full Form

B.Sc. in Agriculture — Bachelor of Science in Agriculture.

Duration

4 years.

Eligibility

12th Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics), usually with at least 50% aggregate marks.

Top Colleges in India

  • Punjab Agricultural University, Ludhiana
  • Tamil Nadu Agricultural University, Coimbatore
  • Acharya NG Ranga Agricultural University, Andhra Pradesh
  • GB Pant University of Agriculture & Technology, Uttarakhand
  • Chandra Shekhar Azad University of Agriculture and Technology, Kanpur
  • Banaras Hindu University (BHU), Varanasi
  • Jawaharlal Nehru Krishi Vishwavidyalaya, Jabalpur

Admission

Through entrance exams like ICAR AIEEA, state-level, or university-level exams; some colleges offer merit-based admissions.

Fees

₹20,000 – ₹1,00,000 per year (government colleges); ₹1,00,000 – ₹2,50,000 per year (private colleges).

Career Opportunities

  • Agricultural Officer
  • Farm Manager
  • Agronomist
  • Agricultural Scientist
  • Plantation Manager
  • Agriculture Loan Officer
  • Seed Technologist
  • Food Processing Industry
  • Higher studies (M.Sc. Agriculture, MBA Agri-Business, etc.)

Salary in India

₹3 lakh – ₹5 lakh per year (starting); can go up to ₹6 lakh – ₹12 lakh or more with experience.

3) B.Sc. in Agriculture Admission Process (Steps)

  • Step 1: Check eligibility (12th Science with required subjects).
  • Step 2: Apply for national/state/university entrance exams (e.g., ICAR AIEEA).
  • Step 3: Appear for entrance exam and qualify.
  • Step 4: Check merit list or counseling schedule.
  • Step 5: Attend counseling and verify documents.
  • Step 6: Get seat allotment and confirm admission.
  • Step 7: Pay fees and join the program.

Summary

B.Sc. in Agriculture is a 4-year professional degree focusing on agriculture and allied sciences. It provides a strong foundation for careers in government, agribusiness, research, and higher studies. Admissions are through entrance exams or merit, with good future prospects in the agricultural sector.
